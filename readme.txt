== Description ==

The No-Code Solution to Launch a Generative NFT Collection On your Website. 

KoalaMint takes care of the entire process to launch your generative NFT collection directly on your website, without technical knowledge required.


CREATE YOUR GENERATIVE NFT COLLECTION WITH
---------------------

* MetaMask Integration
* Smart Contract Creation
* Assets and Metadata to IPFS (decentralized storage technology)
* Add Mint Button on your Website
* Posterior NFT revealing
* Showcase your collection on OpenSea
* Generative Art Creation (coming soon)


AVAILABLE NETWORKS
---------------------

* Ethereum
* Polygon
* Solana (coming soon)


HOW IT WORKS
---------------------

1. Create your account in [KoalaMint](https://koalamint.com/)
2. Create your generative NFT collection by uploading your assets to our platform via a Google Drive integration.
3. Deploy your ERC-721 smart contract with a click on our platform.
4. Add the mint button to your WordPress website via a shortcode and start selling your NFTs. 
5. Reveal your collection at a later date.

== Installation ==

1. Unpack the `download-package`.
2. Upload the file to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
3. Activate the plugin through the `Plugins` menu in WordPress.
4. Done and Ready.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I create the NFT collection on my WordPress website? =
* No, the collection will be created in our platform and you'll be able to add a mint button on your WordPress website (posts/pages) via a shortcode.

= Why do I need an API key? =
* In order to add the mint button on your website, we need to link your NFT collection created on our platform with your website.

= Is the plugin free? =
* Yes, it is. However, the use of our platform has a fixed cost to deploy your smart contract and a small fee percentage per NFT successful sale. Learn more about our pricing [here](https://koalamint.com/pricing/).

= Do you have more doubts about the service? =
* Please, head to our [Help Center](http://support.koalamint.com/) to see a complete and updated list of FAQs.
